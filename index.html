EcommerceU Funnel Analysis

A funnel analysis in an eCommerce company is used to track the customer journey through the sales process. It allows the business to identify where customers are dropping out of the sales process, and determine which areas need improvement. The funnel typically includes metrics such as the number of visitors, time spent on the website, cart abandonment rate, checkout conversion rate, and customer lifetime value. By tracking these metrics, the company can identify areas where customers are getting stuck and take action to improve the customer experience.
